The management of cloud applications and cloud services is a fundamental aspect of using cloud platforms including Softlayer and Bluemix. This covers the deployment and maintenance of applications and services, with features such as continuous availability and seamless updating. In addition there is the monitoring of applications and services both at the technical and at the business level, with autonomic adjustment and correction for any problems discovered.

Building dashboards and integrating with management tooling for cloud applications and services are well understood by Emerging Technologies.
