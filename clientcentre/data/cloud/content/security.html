Security is a key consideration for customers of cloud services. Security covers a range of capabilities - from ID and Access Management, confidentiality of data both in motion and at rest, data integrity and data availability. Handling of Personally Identifiable Information ("PII") is part of this.

Emerging Technologies has expertise and experience in Security and is able to assist in creating effective solutions for cloud services and the applications which are based on them. Examples include "frictionless security" for mobile devices, aiming to reduce the intrusion of security requirements into the experience of end-users, with graduated requirements for proof of identity based on the nature of the operations and the data being accessed.
