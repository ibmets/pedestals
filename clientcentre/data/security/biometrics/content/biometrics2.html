Biometrics Ageing

Biometrics ageing is a very interesting problem that has been looked at by research and we still have a lot of open questions. We are currently doing research around Biometrics ageing factors/implications and investigating the open problems with it. We are also looking at the concerns/limitations of ageing that could affect various Industries and working closely with IBM Research US and University of Kent in solving the open problems in Biometrics ageing.
