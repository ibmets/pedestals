We entered the iDASH Privacy and Security Workshop 2015 competition. The challenge was to compare two given genomes via Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) and find the Hamming and Edit distance (competition defined).


The current incarnation of our scheme runs under 5 minutes (not including network latency) on VCF files (a standard format used by genomicists to encode genomes) of 100k entries on a 4 core CPU with 8GB of RAM.
