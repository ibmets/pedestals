IBM VANI and BluePages+


IBM VANI (Voice Activated Natural Interface) was a joint project between ETS the IBM CIO Office and IBM Research. VANI is a mobile app that enables speech querying of the IBM Faces and Answers services, allowing hands free lookup of employee information and voice search of the Answers Q&A service.

Automatic speech recognition and text to speech services were provided by the research iTrans platform service using custom domain specific language models built from information mined from the faces and answers databases.

The VANI web client has new been reskinned and an updated version

IBM Watson Speech SDK for iOS and Android


An output of the joint VANI project was a mobile speech SDK for use with the IBM Watson Bluemix Speech services. The SDK features support for Speech to Text and Text to Speech using a variety of language models and voices. It has audio compression capabilities and streams audio for recognition using the WebSockets protocol.

Voice IoT


The Voice IoT project uses speech to text capabilities to provide a more human friendly interface for controlling home automation solutions, by combining natural language processing and speech recognition it is possible to interpret voice commands and take appropriate actions. Imagine for example being able to simply say "I'm cold" and have the house central heating raise the temperature to warm you up. Or simply state, "I'm going out for an hour" and letting the Voice IoT handle conserving power and heat while you are out.
