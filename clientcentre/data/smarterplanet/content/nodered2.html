Node-RED is used extensively both inside and outside of IBM, and is now a mainstay of Emerging Technology's rapid prototyping capability.

It enables the integration of local services (e.g. networks, files, hardware interfaces, operating system services and APIs, and databases etc.), online services (Twitter, IRC, XMPP Chat, RSS/ATOM and Email etc.), and processing functions (e.g. JavaScript, sentiment analysis, etc.)

The use of Node-RED enables the developer to focus on the function of the solution rather than the low level integration.
