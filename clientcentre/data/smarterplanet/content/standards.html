Emerging Technology contributes to the development of standards within and across industries. We have particular involvement in the development of the MQTT lightweight publish-subscribe protocol (and its open source implementations) that is becoming a key component of IoT solutions.

Our Edgware Fabric IoT integration technology (built on MQTT) forms the basis of the UK MoD's standard node for edge of network integration (DefStan 23-14). We also supported the development of the MoD's Generic Vehicle Architecture standard (DefStan 23-09).
