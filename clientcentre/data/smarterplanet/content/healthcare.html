Emerging Technology has a long history with applying/developing home automation technologies, and developing assisted living solutions that support elderly, infirmed and disabled people in retaining their independence and safety at home. Most notable amongst these is the Bolzano Safer Living project.

We are presently supporting a partner with the development of a commercial system in the same vein as Bolzano.

Z-Wave is a promising and relatively low cost wireless home automation technology with a healthy and innovative eco-system of device manufactures. Emerging Technology has demonstrated how Z-Wave devices combined with the Raspberry Pi platform, and powered by Node-RED, could be the foundation of a sophisticated but cost effective solution for homes and building.
