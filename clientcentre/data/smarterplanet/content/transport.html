Smarter Commute is an example of a travel and transport solution developed by Emerging Technology. This is a trusted system to allow car sharing within an enterprise/organisation, connecting drivers and passengers in realtime using their smartphones.

The system is not limited to just cars and can be integrated into public transport services too, giving passengers many more options for getting to and from work. Smarter Commute can give enterprises an objective measure of their contribution to lowering greenhouse gas emissions, and reducing traffic congestion in the local area.

Moreover, Smarter Commute can be integrated into building management systems by giving enterprises greater insight into the realtime occupancy of their buildings.
