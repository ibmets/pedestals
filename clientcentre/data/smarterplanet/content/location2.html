Emerging Technology has been looking at two approaches to indoor location. The first uses existing WiFi technology to determine location, based upon the triangulation of signal strength relative to known locations of WiFi hot spots. The second involves the detection of proximity to emitters, often based on low power Bluetooth devices commonly called iBeacons.

Example: Emerging Technology is working with retail clients and IBM's recently released Presence Zones product, aimed at enabling retailers to transform their in-store customer experience by using location based technology to engage shoppers in near real time.
