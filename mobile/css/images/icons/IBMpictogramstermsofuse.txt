IBM pictogram collection: terms of use

This collection of pictograms, created exclusively for IBM, can be used in IBM projects worldwide and in most media (except for a few limitations listed below). The images may be used by IBM employees and agencies working on behalf of IBM in:
--Presentations, including Symphony and PowerPoint
--Film and video formats 
--Advertising and promotional projects 
--Publications, such as newsletters, magazines, emails and editorials
--Websites, intranet sites and apps
--Printed materials including collateral or materials produced for events

Limitations: These images should not be:
--Provided to non-employees except for agency partners
--Used as part of a trademark or logo
--Used in an unflattering, controversial, or offensive context
--Used by third parties such as clients, business partners or in the context of comarketing or cobranding alliances
